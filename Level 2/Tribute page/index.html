About Jensen Huang

Jensen Huang is a visionary entrepreneur, innovator, and co-founder of NVIDIA, a company at the forefront of AI and GPU technology. Born in Taiwan and raised in the United States, Jensen has been instrumental in transforming NVIDIA from a graphics company into a global leader in computing and artificial intelligence. Under his leadership, NVIDIA pioneered GPU computing, revolutionizing industries like gaming, healthcare, automotive, and more. Jensen’s relentless pursuit of innovation, coupled with his inspiring leadership style, has made him one of the most respected figures in technology.

Achievements

  • Founder and CEO of NVIDIA, a global leader in AI and GPUs.
  • Recipient of IEEE Founder’s Medal and other prestigious awards.
  • Developed CUDA, a groundbreaking platform for parallel computing.
